AUSTRALIAN CENTRE FOR CONTEMPORARY ARTIAN POTTER CENTRESomething Happened HereHere celebrates the creativity found around Melbourne. This project by the City of Melbourne, wants to teach both Melbournians and tourists visiting the city about where creativity was born and raised. Around the city, you will find Here markers, celebrating something happened here… By downloading the app, you will be able to tour the city, find the hidden markers projected onto various notable sites, scan the QR code and learn what happened there. This project will allow you the chance to discover a new side of Melbourne you never knew about, discover sites that are passed and ignored every day, and hopefully discover something new.
